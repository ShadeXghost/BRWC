Road to 2026

A playable roadmap where the collection grows into community chaos, match-day rewards, token utility and physical collectibles.

PHASE 01

Collection Launch

BRWC begins with a surreal NFT collection inspired by World Cup culture, national energy, memes and hyper-detailed 3D brainrot creatures.

Launch the core collection, open the website, establish the visual universe, connect the project to OpenSea and begin building the first base of holders.

PHASE 02

Community Chaos

The community becomes part of the game through memes, national rivalries, holder events, votes, quests and football-themed campaigns.

Turn holders into active participants with memes, votes, mini-games, fan wars, country rivalries and social campaigns that keep the project alive.

PHASE 03

Rewards Pool

A percentage of creator rewards may be directed into the BRWC Community Rewards Pool.

The pool may be used for future holder rewards, token campaigns, events, collabs and ecosystem growth. This becomes the fuel behind the game.

PHASE 04

Match-Day Rewards

NFTs connected to national teams may unlock bonuses when those teams win, score or advance in the tournament.

During the World Cup, national team NFTs become part of the live game. Match results may unlock bonuses, community rewards and special campaigns.

PHASE 05

Champion Bonus

Holders of NFTs connected to the winning nation may become eligible for a special Champion Bonus.

Once the World Cup champion is crowned, the winning nation’s NFTs may receive a special bonus funded by the BRWC Community Rewards Pool.

PHASE 06

BRWC Token

The BRWC token expands the ecosystem with community incentives, holder rewards, voting, quests and future utility.

The token becomes the ecosystem fuel for participation, incentive mechanics, community actions, future access and deeper collection utility.

PHASE 07

Physical Collectibles

Selected characters and NFTs may evolve into physical collectibles, turning digital brainrot into real-world objects.

The long-term vision includes transforming the universe into physical pieces such as collectible figures, cards, packs or special objects inspired by BRWC.
